> ##### 🌐 The Big Book of R
>
>**Link:** [https://www.bigbookofr.com/](https://www.bigbookofr.com/) 
>
><i>Oscar Baruffa</i>
>
>This resource brings together a curated collection of R programming books in a single location, eliminating the need to search across multiple sources. Originally launched in August 2020 with approximately 100 books collected over two years, the collection has since grown through continued curation and community contributions to include more than 400 books. Most of the books are freely available and open source, while a smaller number are paid but generally offered at affordable prices.
