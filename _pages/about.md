# Section on Complexity and Network Studies

Within the broad aims and purposes of the American Society of Public Administration (ASPA), the Section on Complexity and Network Studies (SCNS) promotes the professional development of studies on complexity, systems, and networks in the public sector as it relates to both the scholarly and practical needs of the field. Embracing both theoretical and practical concerns, CNS addresses issues in empirical theory building and the knowledge base of the field as well as practical findings, which help meet challenges faced in public administration.
